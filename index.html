J. Luna Zagorac

I am a fifth year Ph.D. Candidate in Physics at Yale University primarily working on theoretical particle cosmology and HPC simulations of dark matter. I am co-advised by Nikhil Padmanabhan at Yale and Richard Easther at the University of Auckland in New Zealand. I am also a Future Investigator in NASA Earth and Space Science and Technology (FINESST), which will fund my graudate thesis on simulations and observational signatures of ultralight (or fuzzzy) dark matter.
