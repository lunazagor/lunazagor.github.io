J. Luna Zagorac

/jovənə ˈluːnə zɑgorəts/ | يوفانا لونا زاغوراتس | Јована "Луна" Загорац

I am a fifth year Ph.D. Candidate in Physics at Yale University primarily working on theoretical particle cosmology and HPC simulations of dark matter. My graduate thesis is focused on HPC simulations and observational signatures of ultralight (or fuzzy) dark matter. I am beingco-advised by Nikhil Padmanabhan at Yale and Richard Easther at the University of Auckland in New Zealand. I am also a Future Investigator in NASA Earth and Space Science and Technology (FINESST).
